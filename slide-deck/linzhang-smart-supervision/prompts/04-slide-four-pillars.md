# 04-slide-four-pillars

## Style
Corporate executive briefing slide, bright professional technology style for county natural resources leadership. White and very light blue background, deep technology blue primary color, cyan accent, dark gray text. Subtle faded satellite imagery texture or GIS map grid background with white translucent overlay. Clean rounded cards, thin blue separators, restrained government-report tone. No cartoon style, no dark background, no excessive colors. 16:9 presentation slide. Chinese text must be crisp and readable.

## Slide Content
Title: 总体思路：四梁体系
Main message: 一底座、一核心、一抓手、一闭环。
2x2 architecture cards: 一底座：时空数据智能开放平台; 一核心：套合监管系统; 一抓手：卫星遥感 + 低空巡查; 一闭环：自然资源业务监管流程. Bottom flow line: 数据汇聚 → 智能识别 → 疑似图斑 → 自动套合 → 属性研判 → 人工核查 → 整改复核 → 台账归档.

## Negative Constraints
No dark navy full background. No cartoon. No excessive orange/green/purple. No crowded text. No fake brand logos. Avoid overpromising AI automatic law enforcement. Do not mention mining.
