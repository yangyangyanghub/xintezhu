# 同等学力英语作文通用模板

## 一、近 10 年题型规律

这些作文基本可以分成 4 类：

### 1. 利弊分析类

典型题目：

- online learning 的优缺点
- self-employed jobs 的优缺点
- machine translation 能否取代人工翻译
- artificial intelligence 是否可怕

核心写法：

> 现象引入 → 承认优点 → 分析问题 → 给出个人观点

这是最高频、最适合套模板的题型。

### 2. 观点讨论类

典型题目：

- Is winning the most important?
- Why pursue graduate studies?
- Should we fear artificial intelligence?

核心写法：

> 提出现象 → 表明观点 → 给出 2-3 个理由 → 总结升华

### 3. 方法建议类

典型题目：

- How can we contribute to environmental protection?
- Ways to live a simpler life

核心写法：

> 问题重要性 → 提出方法一、二、三 → 呼吁行动

### 4. 描述说明类

典型题目：

- The ideal public library
- What makes happy couples happy?

核心写法：

> 说明对象重要性 → 描述特点 → 举例说明 → 总结意义

## 二、最推荐背的通用模板

这套模板适合绝大多数题目，尤其是：

- 科技类
- 教育类
- 社会现象类
- 利弊类
- 观点类

字数约 170-190 词，考试够用。

## Universal Essay Template

Nowadays, with the rapid development of society, the topic of **【主题】** has aroused wide public concern. Some people believe that **【主题】** brings many benefits, while others worry that it may cause some problems. As far as I am concerned, we should look at this issue in a reasonable and balanced way.

On the one hand, **【主题】** has several obvious advantages. First of all, it can make our life more convenient and efficient. For example, **【举例】**. In addition, it provides people with more choices and opportunities. This is especially important in modern society, where people are facing increasing pressure and competition.

On the other hand, we should not ignore its possible disadvantages. If people depend too much on **【主题】**, they may lose some important abilities, such as independent thinking, communication skills or self-control. Besides, without proper rules and guidance, **【主题】** may also bring negative effects to individuals and society.

In my opinion, **【主题】** itself is neither good nor bad. What really matters is how we use it. Therefore, we should make full use of its advantages and try our best to avoid its disadvantages. Only in this way can we benefit from **【主题】** and create a better life.

## 三、中文意思

如今，随着社会的快速发展，**【主题】** 已经引起了广泛关注。有些人认为它带来很多好处，也有人担心它会造成一些问题。在我看来，我们应该理性、全面地看待这个问题。

一方面，**【主题】** 有明显优势。首先，它能让生活更方便、更高效。比如 **【举例】**。此外，它还给人们提供了更多选择和机会，这在现代社会尤其重要，因为人们面临越来越大的压力和竞争。

另一方面，我们也不能忽视它可能带来的缺点。如果人们过度依赖 **【主题】**，可能会失去一些重要能力，比如独立思考、沟通能力或自我控制。此外，如果缺乏规则和引导，它也可能对个人和社会产生负面影响。

在我看来，**【主题】** 本身没有绝对好坏，关键在于我们如何使用它。因此，我们应该发挥它的优势，同时尽量避免它的缺点。只有这样，我们才能从中受益，创造更好的生活。

## 四、不同题目怎么套

### 1. AI / 机器翻译 / 在线学习

主题替换：

> artificial intelligence / machine translation / online learning

举例句：

> For example, online learning enables students to study at home and arrange their time more freely.

或：

> For example, machine translation helps people understand foreign languages quickly.

### 2. 环境保护

把第二段改成方法型：

> First of all, we should save water and electricity in our daily life. In addition, we can use public transportation instead of private cars. More importantly, everyone should develop the habit of protecting the environment.

结尾主题替换：

> environmental protection

### 3. 简单生活

主题替换：

> living a simpler life

举例句：

> For example, people can spend less money on unnecessary things and pay more attention to family, health and personal growth.

### 4. 考研 / 读研原因

主题替换：

> pursuing graduate studies

举例句：

> For example, graduate studies can help students gain professional knowledge and improve their competitiveness in the job market.

### 5. 幸福伴侣

主题替换：

> a happy relationship

举例句：

> For example, happy couples often communicate with each other honestly and solve problems together.

## 五、考场最稳结构

建议按这个 4 段写：

```text
第一段：引出话题 + 表明要理性看待

第二段：写优点 / 原因 / 方法一二

第三段：写缺点 / 问题 / 注意事项

第四段：总结观点 + 升华
```

万能逻辑：

> 不是绝对好，也不是绝对坏，关键看怎么使用 / 怎么对待。

这个逻辑能套 AI、机器翻译、在线学习、自雇、竞争、读研、环保，覆盖面很大。

## 六、必背万能句

### 开头句

> Nowadays, with the rapid development of society, the topic of ... has aroused wide public concern.

### 表达观点

> As far as I am concerned, we should look at this issue in a reasonable and balanced way.

### 写优点

> On the one hand, ... has several obvious advantages.

### 写缺点

> On the other hand, we should not ignore its possible disadvantages.

### 总结句

> What really matters is how we use it.

### 升华句

> Only in this way can we benefit from it and create a better life.

## 七、考试建议

这类作文不要追求高级花活，稳最重要。

核心套路：

> 现象出现了，有好处，也有问题。我们要理性看待，发挥优点，避免缺点。

这个套路能覆盖 80% 以上题目。作文不是写成莎士比亚，是让阅卷老师觉得：结构清楚、语法稳定、字数够、观点不偏。
